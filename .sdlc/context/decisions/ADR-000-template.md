# ADR-000 — Architecture Decision Record Template

## Status

PROPOSED | ACCEPTED | SUPERSEDED | DEPRECATED

Superseded by: (ADR-NNN if applicable)

---

## Context

Describe the situation that forces a decision. Include: what the system is doing, what
constraint or requirement is creating pressure, and what the consequences of not deciding are.
Be specific — name the files, components, and failure modes involved.

---

## Decision

State the decision in one sentence using the imperative voice.

> We will use X instead of Y for Z.

---

## Rationale

Explain why this option was selected over the alternatives. Address:
- What alternatives were considered
- Why each alternative was rejected
- What constraints made this the correct choice (performance, maintainability, team capability, cost)

Do not restate the decision. Explain the engineering reasoning behind it.

---

## Consequences

### Positive
- List the direct benefits

### Negative / Trade-offs
- List what is made harder, more expensive, or constrained by this decision

### Neutral
- List structural changes with no clear valence

---

## Implementation Notes

Where does this decision manifest in the codebase? Name specific files, directories,
configuration keys, or patterns that encode this decision.
